Cebu - Bohol Educational Tour

Holy Cross of Davao College, BS in Information Technology, Cebu - Bohol Educational Tour November 12 - 15, 2025. During our educational tour, we had the opportunity to visit several companies and organizations across Cebu and Bohol. Each stop offered a different perspective on technology, innovation, public service, and the evolving IT landscape in the Philippines. These are my reflections based on each visit:

Educational Tour Journal

Here is a complete documentation of the 5 companies we visited during our educational tour, observations, and learnings.

Facilitators

Name: Sir Arvin Lauron / Sir Kent Vincent Sarsalejo

Role: Tour Facilitator

Company 1: UP BUSINESS INCUBATOR FOR IT

Observations: UP Cebu’s IT Incubator supports startups with business management, technology transfer, finance, mentorship, and internships connecting Visayas, Leyte, and Mindanao.

Learnings: I realized that startups need guidance and resources. Ideas can come from daily experiences. The 3R’s remix, recombine, reinvent encourage creativity. AI enhances business and products.

Company 2: RIVAN IT
CEBU

Observations: Rivan IT empowers IT learners through Cyber Security, Network Engineering, and DevOps programs.

Learnings: Continuous learning is vital. Community-driven training bridges education and real-world IT careers.

Company 3: DYNATA PHILIPPINES INC.

Observations: Dynata emphasized OSH laws, emergency planning, and global IT operations.

Learnings: Workplace safety and IT system reliability are essential in global business operations.

Company 4: MATA TECHNOLOGIES, INC.

Observations: Mata focuses on AR/VR, 360 media, e-commerce, and creative IT solutions.

Learnings: Versatility, teamwork, and adaptability are crucial in tech and creative industries.

Company 5: T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117

Observations: A 24/7 emergency response unit using coordination and communication systems.

Learnings: Preparedness, teamwork, and proper systems save lives.
